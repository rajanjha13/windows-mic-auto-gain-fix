# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any abusive, discriminatory, or hateful conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or edit comments, commits, code, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Contact

For concerns, open a discussion or contact the maintainer privately.
