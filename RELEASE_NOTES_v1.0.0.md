# Mic Volume Guard v1.0.0

Mic Volume Guard helps Windows users stop random microphone auto-gain and sudden volume drops during calls and recordings.

## Highlights
- Lock microphone system volume to your chosen level.
- Automatically restore volume when apps try to change it.
- Use a compact always-on-top overlay to monitor mic level.
- Click `+` or `-` on the overlay to adjust mic volume instantly.
- Enable startup with Windows from settings.

## Best for
- Google Meet
- Steam voice chat
- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams
- Discord
- OBS and other recording workflows

## Download
- `MicVolumeGuard-Windows-win-x64.zip`
- Extract and run `MicVolumeGuard.exe`

## Known limitation
The app enforces Windows microphone endpoint volume. Some apps can still apply their own internal DSP/AGC inside their audio pipelines.
